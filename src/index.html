Hello! I’m Jagoda, a digital designer with a background in photography. My primary focus lies in web and visual design, where I draw inspiration from my passion for elegant simplicity and minimalism. Each project reflects my commitment to combining a user-centered approach with timeless design that transcends trends.

Throughout my journey, I have had the pleasure of collaborating with a diverse array of clients—including agencies, studios, and fellow freelancers. Currently, I’m freelancing while also working part-time for the GoodKarma Agency.

Beyond design, I am deeply passionate about modern art and analogue photography. These interests continually inspire my work, fueling my desire to explore new ideas and push creative boundaries.
