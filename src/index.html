Hello! I’m Jagoda, a UX/UI designer specialising in e-commerce, with a background in photography and a passion for creating meaningful, user-centred digital products. I design thoughtful platforms and interfaces that connect people with brands while balancing business goals and user needs.

My work sits at the intersection of visual design, interaction, and UX strategy. Driven by elegant simplicity and timeless aesthetics, I aim to craft interfaces that feel intuitive, purposeful, and enduring.

I’ve collaborated with agencies, studios, and freelancers on a wide range of projects and currently split my time between freelance work and a part-time role at GoodKarma Agency in Amsterdam.

Outside of work, I find inspiration in modern art and photography, which continually shape how I think about design.
